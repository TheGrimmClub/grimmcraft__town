# Minecraft Datapack Fireworks

## Origin 

We have a yearly tradition to do our fireworks in Minecraft.
After crafting rockets by hand in the first year, we started a datapack that was added to year by year.

It is a nice little project to lighten up your dark days and keep your footprint small.

## Motivation

Building a Minecraft fireworks is a team effort. 
- Building or finding a great vicinity, improving it.
- Starting on local stuff: A big year build that will be removed on the end of the year?
- Placing rocket launchers (command blocks), working with visual effects.
- Executing the fireworks - just for fun or recording it.
And endless more.

Kids and teens can learn much if they interact, plan and execute.
And in the end we have a celebration - the fireworks.

My teens these days started in October to spin up a new server, decide on a vicinity
and collect people to help.

In the first year I was the project leader, the server admin and the idea giver.
Nowadays I am just part of the work force and it is really fun to work together with my teens, kids and their friends.
